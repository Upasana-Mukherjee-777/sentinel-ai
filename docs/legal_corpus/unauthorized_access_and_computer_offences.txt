IT Act 2000, computer-related offences (illustrative summary, verify
current text before citing in any real filing): unauthorized access to
a computer, computer system, or network, and causing wrongful loss or
damage through such access, is a punishable offence. This is the
relevant category for cases involving phishing websites that harvest
login credentials, malicious QR codes that redirect to credential-
harvesting pages, and unauthorized transactions initiated after such
credential theft.
