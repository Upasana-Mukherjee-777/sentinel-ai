Recommended investigation steps for digital arrest / impersonation scam
complaints (illustrative, based on common cyber cell practice, not a
legal provision): (1) preserve the call recording or screen-recording
provided by the complainant as evidence with a documented chain of
custody, (2) identify the originating phone number and check it against
the Fraud Network Graph for prior linked reports, (3) trace any payment
made during or after the call through the receiving UPI ID or bank
account, again cross-checked against the Fraud Network Graph for mule
account patterns, (4) if a fake video-call "arrest" was staged, request
any deepfake analysis available on the recording, (5) advise the
complainant that no genuine Indian law enforcement or government agency
conducts arrests, investigations, or demands payment over a phone or
video call.
