Provisions relating to counterfeit currency (illustrative summary, verify
current BNS section numbering before citing in any real filing):
possessing, using, or trafficking in currency known to be counterfeit,
and the making or manufacturing of counterfeit currency, are treated as
serious offences under Indian criminal law. Complaints involving a
citizen or merchant identifying a counterfeit note through the
Counterfeit Currency Agent should be escalated with the flagged note
retained as physical evidence and photographed with the agent's
highlighted-region output attached to the case file.
